Site plan

Site Name : Green blue Planet

Domain name: greenblueplanet.world

Site purpose:

This website project promotes and talks about effective environment management and protection.
There will be news, articles, strategies and exchanges related to the environment and climate conservation.

Scenario: For Green Blue Planet

  • What missions for Green Blue Planet project?
  • Why we should care for the environment?
  • What are the environment degradation causes?
  • Who are concerned about environment protection.
  • Which programs to promote in order to protect the environment?
  • How to sign up for the program?
  • What tips for caring for the environment?
  • Where to find communities and means to promote and talks about environmental care and exchanges?
